**Attentionï¼š**

- Any fish, except for tropical fish, will take damage when in the HotBath block. (Octopuses will also receive damage).
- Aquatic creatures will not spawn automatically in the HotBath block.
- Coral Plant Blocks, Conduit Blocks, Top Plant Blocks, and Sea Grass Blocks cannot be placed in HotBath blocks.
- Fishing in the HotBath block will not yield any items.
